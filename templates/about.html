About Makokha Medical Centre

Trusted healthcare in Maliki for over 15 years

Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 2009, Makokha Medical Centre has been dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare services to the Maliki community and surrounding areas. Our mission is to deliver compassionate, comprehensive, and accessible medical care to every patient.

We envision a healthier community where quality healthcare is accessible to all, regardless of background or circumstance. Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to make this vision a reality.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a patient-centered approach, we strive to be the leading healthcare provider in our region, setting standards for excellence in medical care.

2015

Today

Serving over 50,000 patients with comprehensive healthcare services and community programs.
